Have you heard about Paul Buchhneit?

Paul Buchheit is an American computer programmer and businessman, he is the main creator and developer of Gmail and he developed the original Google AdSense prototype as part of his work on Gmail.

He also suggested the famous company slogan "Don't be evil" at a meeting in 2000 about company values.

Buchheit grew up in Rochester, New York and went to Case Western Reserve University in Cleveland, Ohio. At Intel and later he worked as Google's 23rd employee. He is currently a venture capitalist and founder of FriendFeed, acquired by Facebook in August 2009 in a private transaction.
